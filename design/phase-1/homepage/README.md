# Phase 1 Homepage Concept

This responsive prototype implements the approved high-fidelity homepage direction without creating the production application or inventing catalog products, prices, testimonials, metrics, or company claims.

## Open locally

Open `homepage.html` in a browser. The page uses only local CSS, JavaScript, and approved brand assets.

## Included

- Desktop and mobile navigation
- Editorial hero with student and business paths
- Approved launch-category presentation
- Ready-made and custom-development comparison
- Student and business audience sections
- Verified-payment and protected-delivery process copy
- Trust, policy, company, and founder content from Phase 0
- Responsive behavior for the Phase 1 verification widths
- Visible focus, semantic structure, reduced-motion support, and a skip link

## Intentionally excluded

- Invented systems or prices
- Live checkout, accounts, database content, and admin behavior
- Production contact email until the mailbox decision is approved
- Testimonials, client logos, sales metrics, registration claims, and office details

The production Next.js application will be created in Phase 2 after the Phase 1 design direction is approved.
