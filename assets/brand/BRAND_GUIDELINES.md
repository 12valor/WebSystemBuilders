# WebSystemBuilders Brand Guide

## Approved direction

The approved identity uses a continuous modular `W`. Its connected strokes represent separate software components working as one system. The mark should feel clean, precise, dependable, and quietly technical.

| Asset | Use |

## Typography

Use Geist for the website and wordmark presentation. Keep `WebSystemBuilders` in camel case with no space. Use a medium or semibold weight and restrained negative letter spacing.

## Usage

- Prefer the horizontal dark-background logo in the website header.
- Use the standalone mark for favicons, compact navigation, avatars, and social graphics.
- Preserve clear space around the logo equal to at least the stroke width of the mark.
- Keep the logo flat. Do not add gradients, glow, shadows, bevels, outlines, or animation to the mark itself.
- Do not rotate, stretch, rearrange, or recolor individual strokes outside the approved variants.
- Maintain accessible contrast against the surrounding background.
